Affiliate Program FAQ

How do I join the Worldwide Brands Affiliate Program?
Just complete this simple form

How does the Worldwide Brands Affiliate Program work?
After signing up, you'll be provided with your affiliate ID and you go to the affiliate tools section to get text links, banner ads, and other marketing materials you can use to promote the Worldwide Brands Directory. When someone clicks through your link or banner, a cookie is set to remember that you referred that customer. If they sign up for an account within 90 days of clicking your link and become a member, you'll earn a commission for that referral.

How much can I earn?
You earn 25% of the purchase amount if the customer purchases directly from your affiliate link or banner. If you refer other affiliates to sign up, you will earn 10% of any sales that your sub-affiliates make.

When and how do I get paid?
We have a minimum payout of $125.00. Once your balance reaches that amount, then the 15th of the next month we will pay the earned commission to the method you selected. We pay all affiliates via PayPal.

Does it cost anything to become an affiliate?
No, it is free to join our affiliate program, and there is no minimum sales level or other requirements.

Can I refer myself?
No. If you join our affiliate program and use the affiliate link to buy for your own personal gain, you will not receive a commission.

Do I get free access to the Wholesaler Directory as an affiliate?
No. The one-time fee for a lifetime membership applies to anyone that wants to access the WBI Certified Wholesaler and Dropshipper Directory. So you must purchase to gain access. We can provide you with any marketing items you need, so please email us with your requests.

I'm outside the USA, can I still join your program?
Yes, you can sign up for our program from most countries, but you must have a PayPal account in order to get paid.

Do you provide cookieless tracking?
Yes! We have worked with our third party affiliate program in order to offer cookieless tracking that tracks by IP as well as your affiliate ID.

How do I contact Worldwide Brands if I have questions about the affiliate program?
Contact our affiliate manager directly via email affiliatemgr@worldwidebrands.com
